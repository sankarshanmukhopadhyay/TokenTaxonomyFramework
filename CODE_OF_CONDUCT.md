# Code of Conduct

The Token Taxonomy Framework community should be professional, respectful, and oriented toward constructive standards development.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate with clarity and respect;
- separate technical disagreement from personal criticism;
- document claims and assumptions where possible;
- support reviewable and evidence-based contributions;
- avoid harassment, intimidation, or exclusionary conduct.

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, or discussions that do not meet these expectations.
